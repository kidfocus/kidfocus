Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first task?
  1. Open the KidFocus app and sign in
  2. Tap the "Add Task" button on the parent dashboard
  3. Fill in the task details (title, description, points, due date)
  4. Select which child the task is for
  5. Tap "Create" to save the task
How does the focus timer work?
The focus timer uses the Pomodoro technique to help children stay focused:
  • 25-minute work sessions followed by 5-minute breaks
  • After 4 work sessions, a longer 15-minute break
  • Fun animations and progress indicators
  • Customizable duration for different activities
How do I manage my subscription?
  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Tap "Manage Subscription"
  3. View your current subscription status
  4. Use the "Manage Subscriptions" button to access App Store settings
  5. Cancel or modify your subscription through Apple's subscription management
Can I use KidFocus with multiple children?
Yes! KidFocus is designed for families with multiple children:
  • Create individual profiles for each child
  • Assign tasks to specific children
  • Track progress separately for each child
  • View all children's activities in the family dashboard
How do I restore my purchases?
  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Tap "Manage Subscription"
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases"
  4. Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase
  5. Your subscription will be restored automatically
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously:
  • All data is encrypted using Apple's CloudKit
  • Secure authentication through Apple Sign In
  • No personal data is shared with third parties
  • Compliance with privacy regulations (COPPA, GDPR)

Troubleshooting

üîß Common Issues

App won't load

  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store

Can't sign in

  • Ensure you're using the correct Apple ID
  • Check that Apple Sign In is enabled on your device
  • Try signing out and back in

Subscription not working

  • Verify your subscription is active in App Store settings
  • Try restoring purchases
  • Check that you're signed in with the correct Apple ID

Data not syncing

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure iCloud is enabled on your device
  • Try signing out and back in to refresh the connection

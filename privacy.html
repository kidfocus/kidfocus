Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use KidFocus.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • Child profiles and information
  • Tasks and activities you create
  • Progress data and achievements
  • Communication with our support team

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data and analytics
  • Performance and crash reports
  • IP address and general location

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Apple Sign In (authentication data)
  • App Store (purchase information)
  • CloudKit (data synchronization)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the KidFocus service
  • Process your subscription and payments
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Improve our App and develop new features
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Cloud Storage

Your data is stored securely using Apple's CloudKit service, which provides:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure data centers
  • Automatic backups
  • Compliance with privacy regulations

3.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication through Apple Sign In
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and monitoring

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in app operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4.3 Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who help us operate the App:

  • Apple (CloudKit, App Store, Sign In)
  • Analytics providers (for app improvement)
  • Customer support platforms

5. Children's Privacy

5.1 COPPA Compliance

We are committed to protecting children's privacy and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5.2 Parental Consent

For children under 13, we require parental consent before collecting personal information. Parents can:

  • Review their child's information
  • Request deletion of their child's data
  • Refuse further collection of information
  • Control their child's account settings

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of certain communications

6.2 Account Settings

You can manage your privacy settings through the App:

  • Update your profile information
  • Control notification preferences
  • Manage child profiles
  • Delete individual tasks or activities
  • Delete your account and all associated data

6.3 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the App:

  1. Open the KidFocus app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Follow the confirmation steps

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. When you delete your account:

  • All your personal data will be permanently deleted
  • All your tasks, activities, and progress will be removed
  • If you are a parent, all child accounts will also be deleted
  • You will need to create a new account if you want to use the app again

If you have a subscription, you should also cancel it in your Apple ID settings to avoid future charges.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the KidFocus service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our App may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app usage
  • Improve performance
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookie settings through your device settings.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the App
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying a notice in the App

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@kidfocus.app

Support: support@kidfocus.app

Website: kidfocus.app

12. Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your personal information includes:

  • Consent (for optional features and communications)
  • Contract performance (to provide the KidFocus service)
  • Legitimate interests (to improve our service and ensure security)
  • Legal obligations (to comply with applicable laws)

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it.

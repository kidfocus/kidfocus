Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address (for parents) or a login code (for children).

Task Data: We store the tasks you create, assign, and complete within the app. This includes task titles, descriptions, due dates, and completion status.

Progress Data: We track points, badges, streaks, and other progress metrics to help motivate children and allow parents to monitor their children's achievements.

Profile Information: If you choose to add a profile picture, we store it securely using Apple's CloudKit infrastructure.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our family task management services
  • To sync data across your devices using Apple CloudKit
  • To send notifications about tasks and achievements
  • To personalize your experience within the app

Children's Privacy

KidFocus is designed with children's privacy in mind:

  • Children use simple 6-character login codes instead of email addresses
  • No personal information is required from children
  • All data is stored securely and only accessible by the parent account
  • We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of notifications (adjustable in device settings)

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings. When you delete your account, all data is permanently removed from our systems.
